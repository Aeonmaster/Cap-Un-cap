Privacy Policy for Cap Un-cap
Effective date: November 7, 2025
Developer: Mitchell Sherman
Contact: mjsanimes777@gmail.com

1. Overview
Cap Un-cap is a Google Docs add-on that allows users to change the capitalization of selected text (for example, convert uppercase text to lowercase or vice versa). This add-on operates entirely within the user’s Google Docs environment using Google Apps Script. Some features require a paid license.

2. Information We Collect
To provide paid features, Cap Un-cap stores the following information:
- Google account email addresses of users who have purchased a license
- Payment confirmation timestamps

This information is stored in a private Google Sheet managed by the developer. No other personal or document content is collected or transmitted.

3. How Information Is Used
- To verify that a user has purchased a license for premium features
- To manage access to the add-on functionality

4. Data Sharing
We do not share license or payment information with third parties. Payment processing is handled securely by PayPal, and PayPal’s privacy policy governs any information collected during payment.

5. User Control and Access
Users can request removal of their license information by contacting the developer at mjsanimes777@gmail.com. Users retain full control of their Google Docs documents.

6. Security
All license information is stored in Google Sheets and protected by your Google account. Payment information is handled entirely by PayPal and is not stored by the add-on.

7. Children’s Privacy
Cap Un-cap is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated over time. The latest version will always be available at the published URL associated with the add-on.
